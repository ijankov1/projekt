Overview

The seventh generation Toyota Celica, introduced in 1999, represented a culmination of design, performance, and advanced technology. With its futuristic and dynamic design, powerful and efficient engine options, and state-of-the-art features, this generation continued the Celica's legacy as a cutting-edge sports car.

Key Features

  • Futuristic and dynamic design
  • Available in coupe and convertible body styles
  • High-performance and efficient engine options, including two 1.8 inline-fours
  • Front-wheel drive configuration
  • Incorporation of state-of-the-art technology for an unparalleled driving experience

Legacy

The seventh generation Celica showcased Toyota's commitment to pushing the boundaries of sports car design and technology. Its success solidified the Celica's position as a symbol of innovation and excitement on the road.
