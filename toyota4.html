Overview

The fourth generation Toyota Celica, introduced in 1985, continued the legacy of innovation and performance. This generation showcased a more streamlined and modern design, incorporating advanced technology and aerodynamics for an enhanced driving experience.

Key Features

  • Streamlined and modern design
  • Available in coupe and convertible body styles
  • Advanced engine options, including a 1.6, 1.8 and a 2.0 inline-four
  • Front-wheel drive and four-wheel drive configuration
  • Incorporation of cutting-edge technology and safety features

Legacy

The fourth generation Celica solidified Toyota's reputation for producing dynamic and technologically advanced vehicles. Its success contributed to the brand's commitment to staying at the forefront of automotive innovation.
